<p><strong>The Deicing Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Regulating Deicing Systems</li><li>Constant Wattage Deicing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deicing cable market primarily comprises two types: self-regulating and constant wattage deicing systems. Self-regulating deicing systems adjust their heat output based on ambient temperature variations, ensuring efficiency and preventing overheating. In contrast, constant wattage systems maintain a fixed power output, providing consistent heating regardless of temperature changes. Both systems are essential for preventing ice buildup on roofs, sidewalks, and other surfaces, enhancing safety and functionality during winter months while minimizing damage from ice accumulation.</p></p>
