<p><p>The Marine Thermal Cameras market is primarily divided into two types: uncooled and cooled thermal cameras. Uncooled thermal cameras operate at ambient temperatures, utilizing materials that do not require refrigeration, making them smaller and more cost-effective. They are ideal for applications requiring portability. In contrast, cooled thermal cameras use cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and provide higher image quality, making them suitable for long-range and low-light conditions. Each type serves distinct needs within marine surveillance and security applications.</p></p>
