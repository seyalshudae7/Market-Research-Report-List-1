<p><ul><li>Relative Type</li><li>Absolute Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resistive hygrometers market is divided into two main types: relative and absolute. Relative humidity sensors measure the moisture in the air compared to the maximum amount it can hold at a specific temperature, providing a percentage reading. Absolute hygrometers, on the other hand, calculate the actual amount of water vapor present in the air, delivering readings in units such as grams per cubic meter. Each type serves distinct applications, impacting their demand across various industries like meteorology and HVAC.</p></p>
