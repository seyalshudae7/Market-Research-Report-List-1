<p><p>The Industrial Solid State Laser market is segmented into two main types: Pulse Type and Continuous Type. Continuous Type lasers provide a continuous beam of light, suitable for processes like welding and sintering, where sustained energy output is essential. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and versatility in manufacturing and material processing.</p></p>
