<p><p>The large truck-mounted crane market features three primary types: folding boom, telescopic boom, and truss boom cranes. Folding boom cranes offer flexibility and compactness, allowing for efficient maneuvering in tight spaces. Telescopic boom cranes provide extended reach with a series of retractable sections, ideal for lifting at various heights. Truss boom cranes, known for their structural strength, utilize a network of triangles to support heavier loads over greater distances. Each type serves different operational needs in construction and transportation sectors.</p></p>
