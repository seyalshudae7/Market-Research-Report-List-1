<p><ul><li>Brush Motor</li><li>Brushless Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC servo motors market is categorized into two main types: brushed and brushless motors. Brushed motors utilize conventional brushes and commutators for operation, offering simplicity and lower initial costs, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, brushless motors employ electronic controllers to eliminate brushes, enhancing efficiency, lifespan, and performance. While brushed motors are often favored for compact, cost-sensitive applications, brushless motors are increasingly preferred in high-performance, precision-driven environments like robotics and automation due to their reliability and reduced maintenance needs.</p></p>
