<p><strong>The Analog Delay Line Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The analog delay line market is primarily divided into two types: passive and active. Passive delay lines rely on passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors to create signal delays without amplification. These are typically simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, active delay lines utilize amplifying components, such as operational amplifiers, to produce greater bandwidth and improved performance. Active lines are often preferred in applications requiring precise timing and greater control over the delayed signal's characteristics.</p></p>
