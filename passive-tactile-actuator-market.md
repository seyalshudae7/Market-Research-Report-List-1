<p><p>The passive tactile actuator market includes three primary types: Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) actuators, which produce vibrations by rotating an unbalanced mass, creating strong tactile feedback; Linear Resonant Actuators (LRA), which use a moving mass and a spring system to deliver precise, high-frequency vibrations for a more nuanced touch experience; and other specialized actuators, which may combine features or adapt to specific applications, enhancing user interaction in devices like smartphones, wearables, and gaming controllers.</p></p>
