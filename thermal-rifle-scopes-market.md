<p><p>The thermal rifle scopes market is primarily divided into two types: un-cooled thermal imaging and cryogenically cooled thermal imaging. Un-cooled thermal imaging scopes utilize a sensor that operates at ambient temperatures, making them more compact and cost-effective, suitable for a wide range of applications. In contrast, cryogenically cooled thermal imaging scopes employ advanced cooling technology to enhance sensor performance and achieve higher resolution images, resulting in superior detection capabilities, but typically come at a higher price and complexity.</p></p>
