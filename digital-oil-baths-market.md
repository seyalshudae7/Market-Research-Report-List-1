<p><ul><li>Circulating</li><li>Non-Circulating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital oil baths market can be categorized into two main types: circulating and non-circulating systems. </p><p>In a circulating oil bath, heat is efficiently distributed through a continuous flow of oil, providing uniform temperature control for applications like heating delicate materials. In contrast, a non-circulating oil bath relies on static oil, primarily utilizing its thermal properties without circulation. This type is simpler and usually employed for basic heating tasks, offering ease of use but less precise temperature management compared to circulating systems.</p></p>
