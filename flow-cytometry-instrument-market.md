<p><strong>The Flow Cytometry Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell Analyzers</li><li>Cell Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flow cytometry instrument market is primarily categorized into two types: cell analyzers and cell sorters. Cell analyzers are devices that measure and analyze the physical and chemical characteristics of cells as they flow in a fluid stream, providing data on cell populations and their physiological states. Cell sorters, on the other hand, not only analyze cells but also separate them based on specific characteristics, allowing for further study or manipulation of distinct cell types. Both types are essential for research and clinical applications in immunology, oncology, and cell biology.</p></p>
