<p><p>The steel tubular piling pipe market comprises two main types: large diameter piles and micropiles. Large diameter piles are used for heavy-load applications and are capable of transferring significant loads deep into the ground, making them essential for large infrastructure projects. Micropiles, on the other hand, are smaller-diameter piles, ideal for tight and limited access sites, providing structural support with minimal disturbance. Both types play crucial roles in foundation engineering, offering solutions tailored to varying project requirements and soil conditions.</p></p>
