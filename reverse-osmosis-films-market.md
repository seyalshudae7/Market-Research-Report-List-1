<p><p>The Reverse Osmosis Films market can be categorized into three main types: Cellulose-Based, Thin Film Composite, and Others. Cellulose-Based membranes, derived from natural materials, offer good permeation but have limited chemical resistance. Thin Film Composite membranes, made from polyamide, provide higher salt rejection and durability, making them popular in water purification. The Others category includes specialty membranes designed for specific applications, such as industrial processes or healthcare, incorporating various materials and technologies to meet diverse filtration needs.</p></p>
