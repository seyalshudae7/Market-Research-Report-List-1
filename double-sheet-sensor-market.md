<p><strong>The Double Sheet Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unilateral Contact</li><li>No Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The double sheet sensor market can be categorized into two primary types: unilateral contact and no contact sensors. Unilateral contact sensors operate by detecting the presence of double sheets through physical contact, relying on pressure or electrical signals to identify the sheets. In contrast, no contact sensors utilize advanced technologies, such as ultrasonic or electromagnetic waves, to identify double sheets without direct physical interaction. Both types serve critical roles in automatic sheet detection, ensuring operational efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
