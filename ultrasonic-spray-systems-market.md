<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Spray Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary-Type Ultrasonic Spray System</li><li>Flat-Type Ultrasonic Spray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic spray systems are specialized technologies used for precision coating and atomization. The rotary-type ultrasonic spray system utilizes a rotating atomizer, generating a fine mist of liquid through ultrasonic vibrations, ideal for continuous, uniform coatings on curved surfaces. In contrast, the flat-type ultrasonic spray system employs a flat nozzle to produce a consistent spray pattern, suited for coating flat or large surfaces. Both systems enhance efficiency and provide high-quality finishes in various applications, including automotive, electronics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
