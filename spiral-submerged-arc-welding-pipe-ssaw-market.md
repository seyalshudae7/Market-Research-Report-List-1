<p><p>Spiral Submerged Arc-Welding Pipe (SSAW) is a type of welded steel pipe characterized by its helical seam, which is formed by spirally winding a flat strip of steel. SSAW pipes are known for their high strength, resilience, and capacity to withstand extreme conditions, making them ideal for various applications, including oil and gas, construction, and structural purposes.</p><p>The SSAW pipe market is experiencing significant growth due to rising demand from infrastructure development and the energy sector.
