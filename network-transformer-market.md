<p><p>The Network Transformer market is categorized into Single Mouth, Double Mouth, and Multiple Mouth types based on the number of input/output connections they offer. Single Mouth transformers feature one point of entry or exit for electricity, making them suitable for simpler applications. Double Mouth transformers support two connections, allowing for bidirectional power flow and enhancing versatility. Multiple Mouth transformers offer several input/output options, catering to complex systems with high demands for distribution and flexibility, often used in large-scale industrial or utility settings.</p></p>
