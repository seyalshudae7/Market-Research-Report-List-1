<p><ul><li>Chronic Toxicity Testing</li><li>Sub-Chronic Toxicity Testing</li><li>Sub-Acute Toxicity Testing</li><li>Acute Toxicity Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The in vivo toxicology market encompasses various testing types to assess the safety of compounds in living organisms. Chronic toxicity testing evaluates long-term exposure effects, while sub-chronic testing focuses on exposures lasting weeks to months. Sub-acute toxicity testing examines effects from shorter-term exposure, typically days to weeks. Acute toxicity testing assesses immediate effects following a short-term exposure, usually within hours. Each testing type plays a crucial role in understanding potential risks and guiding the safe use of substances in pharmaceuticals and chemicals.</p></p>
