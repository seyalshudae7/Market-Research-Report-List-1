<p><ul><li>Sweet Modulator</li><li>Salt Modulator</li><li>Mouthfeel Modulator</li><li>Masking Modulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flavour modulation market includes various types that enhance taste experiences. Sweet modulators enhance sweetness perception without adding sugar, beneficial for low-calorie products. Salt modulators reduce sodium content while retaining salty flavors, appealing to health-conscious consumers. Mouthfeel modulators improve the texture and richness of foods, creating a more satisfying experience. Masking modulators hide undesirable tastes, like bitterness, allowing for more palatable formulations. Together, these modulators cater to evolving consumer preferences for healthier, better-tasting food and beverage options.</p></p>
