<p><p>Face checkpoint scanners are categorized into three main types: wall-mounted style, stand column style, and gate type. Wall-mounted scanners are installed on walls and offer a space-efficient solution for access control. Stand column scanners are freestanding units that can be placed anywhere, providing flexibility in deployment. Gate type scanners integrate with entry points, often used in security checkpoints, enhancing both security and convenience by enabling seamless passage while verifying individuals' identities. Each type serves distinct operational needs in various settings.</p></p>
