<p><p>The CPVC cement market is categorized based on viscosity and application. Regular-bodied CPVC cements are suitable for smaller pipes and quick repairs, offering ease of application. Medium-bodied options provide a balance between viscosity and workability, ideal for a variety of pipe sizes. Heavy-bodied cements are designed for larger pipes and joints, ensuring a strong bond under more demanding conditions. Extra heavy-bodied cements are specifically formulated for high-pressure applications, providing maximum strength and durability for substantial piping systems.</p></p>
