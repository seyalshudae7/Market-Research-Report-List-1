<p><strong>The Electromechanical Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tactile Switch</li><li>Rotary Switch</li><li>Encoder Switch</li><li>Toggle Switch</li><li>Push Switch</li><li>Detect Switch</li><li>Micro Switch</li><li>Dip Switch</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromechanical switch market comprises various types, each serving distinct functionalities. Tactile switches provide feedback upon actuation, while rotary switches allow for multiple positions in a single device. Encoder switches translate rotational movement into digital signals, and toggle switches offer simple on-off control. Push switches activate circuits with a press, while detect switches signal presence or position. Micro switches are compact and used in safety applications, and dip switches allow for configuration settings. Other market segments include specialty and custom switches.</p></p>
