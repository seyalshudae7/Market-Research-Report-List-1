<p><p>The lithium battery copper foil market is primarily divided into two types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electroplating process, resulting in high purity and uniform thickness, making it ideal for high-performance applications in lithium-ion batteries. In contrast, rolled copper foil is manufactured by rolling solid copper sheets and offers advantages in cost efficiency and flexibility, catering to a wide range of battery applications, particularly in consumer electronics.</p></p>
