<p><p>The conducting polymers market is primarily divided into two segments: electrically conducting and thermally conducting polymers. Electrically conducting polymers are utilized in applications such as sensors, batteries, and antistatic materials, enabling efficient charge flow. In contrast, thermally conducting polymers are designed for heat dissipation in electronic devices and automotive components, enhancing thermal management without compromising lightweight properties. Both types cater to diverse industries, driving innovation and growth in electronics, automotive, and renewable energy sectors.</p></p>
