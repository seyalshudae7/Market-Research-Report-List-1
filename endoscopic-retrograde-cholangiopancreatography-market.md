<p><p>The Endoscopic Retrograde Cholangiopancreatography (ERCP) market comprises two main types of duodenoscopes: fiber duodenoscopes and electronic duodenoscopes. Fiber duodenoscopes use optical fibers for imaging and illumination, providing effective visualization but with limitations in image quality. In contrast, electronic duodenoscopes offer enhanced image clarity and precision through digital sensors, enabling better diagnosis and treatment. The choice between these types hinges on factors such as procedural requirements, patient safety, and advancements in technology within gastrointestinal diagnostics.</p></p>
