<p><p>The Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB) market includes three main boiler types: Subcritical, Supercritical, and Ultra-supercritical CFB boilers. Subcritical CFB boilers operate below critical pressure, achieving efficient combustion at lower temperatures. Supercritical CFB boilers operate above critical pressure, enhancing thermal efficiency and reducing emissions. Ultra-supercritical CFB boilers reach even higher pressures and temperatures, maximizing efficiency and performance. Each type caters to varying energy demands and environmental regulations, supporting sustainable power generation through cleaner and more efficient combustion technologies.</p></p>
